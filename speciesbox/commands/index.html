# Dungeon

Aliases Cooldown Description
dungeon 4h:0m:0s Enter to the dungeon and try to find hidden treasures
Use _dungeon to start adventure in dungeon
Each dungeon will have 25 levels
Each dungeon level will have 7 rooms
Each dungeon level always will have five fight rooms, where you need to defeat enemy. You can run away, but you will loose all of your found items
Each dungeon level always will have one event room, where you need to decide what to do, and there will happen event dependent of your choice
Each dungeon level always will have one boss room, where you need to defeat the boss. After killing boss you will open constant shortcut to the next level
You can exit with saving items that you found in dungeon after cleaning any room
If you will die in dungeon, you will not receive found items

# Use

Aliases Cooldown Description
use, u 0h:0m:5s Use items from your inventory (not equipment)
Use _use food to eat one food and regen your hp, stamina and mana for 15% of maximum (20% with regeneration trait)
Use _use cfood to eat one cooked food and regen your hp, stamina and mana for 25% of maximum (30% with regeneration trait)
Use _use ug to use upgrade gem and upgrade your character level
Use _use hg to use health gem and increase your current health (not max health)
Use _use hg equipment_id to use health gem on equipment to increase this equipment health stat
Use _use sg to use stamina gem and increase your current stamina (not max stamina)
Use _use sg equipment_id to use stamina gem on equipment to increase this equipment stamina stat
Use _use mg to use mana gem and increase your current mana (not max mana)
Use _use mg equipment_id to use mana gem on equipment to increase this equipment mana stat
Use _use pg to use power gem and temporary increase your attack stat
Use _use pg equipment_id to use power gem on equipment to increase this equipment attack stat
Use _use dg to use defense gem and temporary increase your defense stat
Use _use dg equipment_id to use defense gem on equipment to increase this equipment defense stat
Use _use ag to break absolute gem and receive few other type gems
Each gem usage gives xp for character and for alchemy skill. Alchemy skill can increase gems effects. You can use gems only 5 times on one equipment
